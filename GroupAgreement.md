* Respect, cooperation, mutual trust, and openness are paramount to fostering collaboration, learning, and growth within the group.

* Thoroughly talk through any conflicts that may arise and ensure that all sides of the argument have been third. May bring in a third party (instructor, LA) if necessary to guide conflict resolution.

Should you use GenAI at all during group work?
* Okay to use it for asking clarifying questions.

If/when you use GenAI, what expectations do you want to agree on as a group?
* It should be mostly used for consultation only after talking to another person first. If AI is used, make it known that it is being used/consulted so that group members may reference where information is coming from.
